About Me

With my background in Philosophy(BA) and an enduring love for examining and analysing the reasons behind why we do things and think in accepted ways, fusioned with a fascination with technology and how it can alter the way we approach our everyday lives. With the abundance of data and information infused into the modern way of life it seems inevitable that I would want to explore the world of code and computing which all too many people are happy to use with little to no understanding of the underlying laws and practices which govern our interactions today.

Now based in France and throwing myself headfirst into the fascinating and, at times frustrating, world of coding I have experienced the same joy of creating something for others to enjoy which led me to the world of professional cooking. In an effort to improve and evolve my skills I am studying and working on my small personal projects, but to truly begin to understand the professional world of coding I am looking for an entry level position as Python or Web Developer, as such, I will be using this page to exhibit my work and studies.
